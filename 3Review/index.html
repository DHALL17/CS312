Carl Garsbo (Kasbo) landed in Gothenburg at the tail end of 2015, the Swedish producer couldn't sit still.“I felt inspired beyond words,” says Garsbo, riding high from a recent trek with his Counter Records labelmates ODESZA and the promising one-two punch of the wildly expressive Umbrella Club EP and tear duct-tapping “World Away” track, he didn’t waste any time. He got right back in the studio.
