Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practive of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama(breath work), and guided mediatation to foster you mind a body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of expierence. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
